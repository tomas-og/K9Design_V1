Our Story

Professional Grooming You Can Trust

At K9 Design, professional grooming is delivered with care, patience, and a genuine love for dogs. Every appointment is tailored to ensure a calm, stress free experience, allowing each dog to feel comfortable while looking their very best. From small breeds to larger companions, every dog is treated with the same level of attention and respect.

Led by a multi award winning, internationally trained groomer, K9 Design combines expert technique with a gentle, personalised approach. Achievements include Salon Dog Groomer of the Year 2025 and the Spirit of Mary Toland Award, alongside specialist expertise in Asian Fusion styling and precision scissor work. As a proud IPDGA member, the focus remains on maintaining the highest standards in both grooming and animal care, because every dog deserves exceptional treatment.

Most dogs benefit from a full groom every 4–8 weeks, depending on coat type, breed, and lifestyle. Longer-haired and double-coated breeds often need more frequent visits to prevent matting, while short-coated breeds can usually go a little longer between sessions.
